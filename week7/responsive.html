PUBG

PUBG(Players Unknown Battle Ground) is a multiplayer
online game in which a team is formed up of 4 players
battling together for kills, missions and level up. The game
was released in 2017. It has since gained a large player base
around the world.00 players are dropped onto an island and
must scavenge for weapons, equipment, and other resources
while fighting to be the last player or team standing. PUBG
has several maps that players can choose from, each with its
own unique terrain and layout.It has several different game
modes, including solo, duo, and squad play, as well as vari
ous limited-time event modes.
